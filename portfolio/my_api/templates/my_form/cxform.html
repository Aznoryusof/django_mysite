Small Loans Form


Banks around the world receive massive amounts of applications each day for loans. Some of these loans are defaulted, costing banks to lose money, and is an inefficient use of limited credit in the economy. Therefore, there is a need to find a better way to differentiate between loans that are likely to be repayed and those that would not.

This form uses a machine learning model trained on historical data on default rates to predict if an applicant is predicted to have a high risk of default. Fill in the information in the fields, and click submit, to test how it works.
